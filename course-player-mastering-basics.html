What Mastering Actually Is

Understanding the purpose of mastering and how it differs from mixing.

In this lesson

  • ‚Üí The difference between mixing and mastering
  • ‚Üí What mastering can and can't fix
  • ‚Üí When to master your own tracks

Key takeaways

Mastering is the final polish that makes your mix ready for distribution. It's not a magic fix for a bad mix.

Mastering ensures your track sounds consistent with other professional releases and translates well across all playback systems.

You can master your own tracks for demos and personal releases, but consider hiring a professional for commercial releases.
