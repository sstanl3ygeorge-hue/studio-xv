Why this course exists

Your mix decisions are only as good as what you can hear. A bad listening environment leads to bad mix decisions—overcompensating for room issues, misjudging bass, and mixes that don't translate.

Most producers ignore their room and blame their monitors or plugins when mixes don't translate.

This course teaches you to optimize your listening environment—simple improvements that make mixing easier and more accurate.
