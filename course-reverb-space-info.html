Why this course exists

Reverb is one of the most misused effects in mixing.

Too much and your mix sounds washed out. Too little and it feels lifeless. Most producers rely on presets without understanding how reverb actually shapes space.

This course teaches you to use reverb intentionally—creating cohesive space that supports the mix rather than drowning it.
