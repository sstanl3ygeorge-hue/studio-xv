Key takeaways

Reverb simulates the natural reflections that happen when sound bounces off walls, floors, and ceilings. Without it, your mix sounds like it's floating in a void.

Your brain expects to hear reverb because that's how sound works in real life. Dry sounds feel unnatural and disconnected from each other.

Reverb isn't decoration‚Äîit's glue. It places sounds in the same space and creates a sense of depth that makes mixes feel three-dimensional.
