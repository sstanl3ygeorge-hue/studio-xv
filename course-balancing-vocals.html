Why this course exists

Vocals are the most important element in most mixes, but they're also the hardest to get right. Too loud and they dominate. Too quiet and they disappear.

This course shows you how to balance vocals consistently without relying on heavy compression or constant automation.

You'll learn how to listen properly, make intentional level decisions, and understand when your vocals are already working.

Who this is for

This course is for you if:

  • ✓ Your vocals sound either too loud or too quiet
  • ✓ You rely on heavy compression to control vocals
  • ✓ You're unsure when to automate and when to leave it
  • ✓ You want natural-sounding vocal mixes

This course is not for you if:

  • ✕ You're looking for vocal production techniques
  • ✕ You need step-by-step plugin chains
  • ✕ You're brand new to music production
