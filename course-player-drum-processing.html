Introduction to drum processing

Understanding the fundamentals of drum processing and how to approach your drum chain with clarity and purpose.

In this lesson

Key takeaways

Great drum processing starts with great source sounds. No amount of processing can fix poorly chosen or recorded drums.

The order of your processing chain matters more than the individual plugins you use. Start with fundamentals: gain staging, EQ, compression, then enhancement.

Most drums fail because they're over-processed, not under-processed. Learn to recognize when less is more and when to stop tweaking.
