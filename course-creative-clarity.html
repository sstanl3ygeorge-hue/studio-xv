Why this course exists

Most producers don't lack creativity. They struggle to know which ideas serve the track and which distract from it.

This course teaches you how to make creative choices with clarity and purpose.

You'll learn how to recognise what works, remove what doesn't, and trust your instincts without second-guessing.

Who this is for

This course is for you if:

  • ✓ You struggle to know which ideas to keep
  • ✓ You over-complicate your arrangements
  • ✓ You second-guess your creative choices
  • ✓ You want to develop a clear creative voice

This course is not for you if:

  • ✕ You're looking for sound design tutorials
  • ✕ You want genre-specific templates
  • ✕ You're brand new to music production

Course modules

Module 1 — Recognising creative clutter

How to identify when you're adding elements that don't serve the track.
