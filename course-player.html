Decision fatigue explained

Understanding why unlimited options create paralysis and how to structure decisions that lead to finished tracks.

In this lesson

  • ‚Üí Why every option creates a new decision point
  • ‚Üí How decision fatigue shows up in your sessions
  • ‚Üí The 3-move rule for reducing overwhelm
  • ‚Üí When to limit options vs. when to explore

Key takeaways

Every plugin you audition, every preset you cycle through, and every tweak you consider creates a new decision point. The problem isn't the tools‚Äîit's the unlimited options without a framework for choosing.

Decision fatigue happens when you spend more time deciding what to do than actually doing it. The solution isn't faster decisions‚Äîit's fewer decisions that matter more.

The 3-move rule: Allow yourself three intentional changes per session. If none of those moves improve the track, the problem isn't what you're changing‚Äîit's what you're listening for.
