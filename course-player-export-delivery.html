Why this course exists

Most producers spend years learning how to make music, but rarely understand how to deliver it properly. Wrong file formats, incorrect sample rates, or mismatched bit depths can cause your tracks to be rejected by distributors or sound worse than they should.

This course exists to fix that.

Instead of guessing or following outdated advice, you'll learn exactly which settings to use for every situation. Whether you're sending tracks to a mastering engineer, uploading to streaming platforms, or delivering client work, you'll know the right choice every time.
